About the Firm

Dedicated to
Delivering Justice

Pooja Tandon & Associates is a multi-disciplinary law firm founded by Advocate Pooja Tandon and headquartered at Chamber No. 405, Lawyers Chamber Block III, Delhi High Court, New Delhi. Since its establishment in 2012, the firm has earned a reputation as one of the most trusted legal practices at the Delhi High Court.

With over 13 years of professional experience, Advocate Pooja Tandon provides expert legal counsel in civil litigation, criminal defence, family and matrimonial disputes, writ petitions, service matters, motor accident compensation claims, consumer protection cases, and corporate advisory services.

The firm serves individuals, families, government employees, businesses, and corporate entities across Delhi and the National Capital Region (NCR) — including Noida, Gurgaon, Ghaziabad, and Faridabad.

CAT Cases Pension Reinstatement
